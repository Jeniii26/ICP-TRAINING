Getting Around Town

Wherever you need to go, Classic Yellow Taxi is ready to take you there, day or night. We service downtown Angeles and the Greater Balibago Area as well.

Universities and Colleges

Classic Yellow Taxi services to the Universities in Angeles , Holy Angel University, National University and Angeles University with safe rides to students 24/7 for errands, work, late night studying, or nighttime entertainment. Discounts are available to students who show current student ID to the driver.

Tours

We offer sightseeing tours of downtown and historic Angeles. Tours are a minimum of 1 hour and maximum of 2 hours. Call us to customize your tour.
