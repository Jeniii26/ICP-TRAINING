Classic Yellow Taxi

Classic Yellow Taxi is an owner-operated company serving the residents, tourists and business professionals in the city of Angeles specifically surrounding Balibago area. Our fleet of unique classic cars is a luxury everyone can afford!

About Us

Established in 2024, Classic Yellow Taxi has been owned and operated by the Kundole family, starting with a fleet of 2 cars. The Kundole family takes pride in providing friendly, prompt service, with a priority on safety.

Service Area

We service Angeles specifically the Greater Balibago area. We provide service to the airport, universities and hospitals. Special pricing is available on request for trips to areas outside of Balibago.
