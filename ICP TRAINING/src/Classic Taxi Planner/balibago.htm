About Angeles City

History

In 1793, Spanish Colonial officials founded the "Town of Kuliat", in the Angeles area. This site became the City of Angeles over time. Angeles City is one of the largest cities in the province of Pampanga. A common misconception is that Angeles is the capital of Pampanga. The City of San Fernando is the provincial capital of Pampanga, and San Fernando City is the capital of La Union.

Culture

Angeles City has many night and day destinations including the Museo Ning Angeles at Sto. Rosario. Explore the city's vibrant culture and attractions riding the Classic Yellow Taxi.

Sports

Angeles City has a strong sports culture with popular activities including basketball, bardagulan, and volleyball. Various local sports events and leagues keep the city's sports enthusiasts engaged throughout the year.

Events

Annual events in Angeles City include the Tigtigan Terakan Keng Dalan festival, the Sisig Festival, and the Philippine International Hot Air Balloon Fiesta, which attract numerous visitors each year.
