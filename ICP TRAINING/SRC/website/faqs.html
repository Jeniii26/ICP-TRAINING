  • What is Chu Chü Beauty and what makes it different from other makeup brands?
    Chu Chü Beauty is a Korean-inspired makeup brand offering playful yet practical, multi-use products that simplify your beauty routine. Our line is designed to bring fun back to beauty with budget-friendly, high-quality items. Chu Chü is cruelty-free, paraben-free, and caters to everyone—from students to professionals—without compromising on innovation or affordability.
  • Are Chu Chü Beauty products cruelty-free and safe to use?
    Yes! All Chu Chü Beauty products are cruelty-free and formulated without parabens. We’re committed to providing ethical beauty options, so you can feel good about using products that are safe for you and kind to animals.
  • What kinds of products does Chu Chü Beauty offer?
    Chu Chü Beauty offers an extensive range of makeup, including lip tints, multi-use sticks, eyeshadow palettes, brow pencils, and more. Our products are designed to be multi-purpose and user-friendly, perfect for creating versatile looks with ease, whether you're at home or on the go.
  • Are Chu Chü Beauty products affordable?
    Absolutely! We believe in making high-quality makeup accessible to everyone. None of our products cost more than Php 1000, so you can build your beauty collection without breaking the bank.
  • Can I achieve multiple makeup looks with Chu Chü Beauty's products?
    Yes! Chu Chü Beauty's multi-use products, like the Juicy Dew Tint and Multiuse Heart Sticks, are designed to be versatile. You can easily mix and match shades or transition from day to night looks with just a few items from our collection.
